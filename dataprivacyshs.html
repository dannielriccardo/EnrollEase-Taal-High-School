Data Privacy Consent

Republic Act No. 10173, also known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012, is a Philippine law that protects individuals’ personal information in information and communications systems in both government and private sectors.


This law ensures that personal data such as your name, address, contact details, school records, and other identifying information are collected, processed, and stored securely and lawfully.


Under this Act, you have the right to:

  • Be informed about how your data is used
  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion when applicable
  • Be protected against unauthorized disclosure

By agreeing below, you consent to the collection and processing of your personal information solely for legitimate educational and administrative purposes in compliance with RA 10173.
